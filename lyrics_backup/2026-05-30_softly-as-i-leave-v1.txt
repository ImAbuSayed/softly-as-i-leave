[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the heat between us burning slow
Every time you pull me in and let me go
Caught inside the rhythm of your heavy breath
I want to hold you close until there’s nothing left

[Verse 1]
Midnight shadows falling on the hardwood floor
You look at me like you’re waiting for something more
I trace the lines of the moonlight across your skin
We don’t have to speak to let this night begin

[Pre-Chorus]
The air is thick with the secrets we both keep
A quiet fire pulling us down deep
I watch the flicker of the candle in your eyes
Taking me away under the starry skies

[Chorus]
Softly as I leave a whisper on your skin
Softly as I leave the heavy world we’re in
I am pulling you into the heat of my embrace
Getting lost in the quiet of this perfect place

[Post-Chorus]
Softly as I leave
[Echo] (Softly as I leave)

[Verse 2]
I can taste the longing every time we touch
Wondering if feeling this is way too much
The silence is a melody that we compose
Letting every feeling bloom like a midnight rose

[Chorus]

Softly as I leave
[Echo] (Softly as I leave)

[Bridge]
Dancing in the fading amber light
Holding onto feelings in the night
Whispering a promise in the dark
Saeed will always keep this tender spark
Feeling every heartbeat pulling near
Melting all the shadows and the fear

[Spoken]
I want to stay in this moment forever with you
Just close your eyes and let me take you there

[Final Chorus]
